# Experiment: 2026-06-13 S03

## Objective

Measure a shorter direct-callback profile with reduced candidate count.

## Public Data Used

Official competition resources were used through the hosted competition environment. Raw files and local caches are not included in this repository.

## Configuration Summary

The submission focused on single-step callback patterns with a smaller return set. The goal was to lower runtime while preserving variation across destinations and markers.

## Metrics

Public leaderboard score.

## Results

| --- | --- | --- |
| s03 | Complete | 2.765 |

## Observations

The shorter direct-callback profile scored materially below the longer baseline, indicating that candidate compression reduced public coverage.
